ComfortGuard HVAC Membership Terms of Service

Effective Date: August 27, 2025 | Last Modified: August 27, 2025 | Version: 1.1

1. Initial Equipment Assessment & Right to Refuse Service

30-Day Evaluation Period

A mandatory equipment inspection will be scheduled within 30 days of membership enrollment. ComfortGuard reserves the right to cancel membership with a full refund if the equipment is found to pose immediate safety hazards, is beyond economical repair (repair costs exceed 60% of replacement value), exceeds 20 years of age (subject to case-by-case evaluation), has been improperly maintained creating excessive liability risk, or contains code violations or unsafe modifications.

Equipment Disqualification Criteria

  • Cracked heat exchangers or other combustion safety issues.
  • Major refrigerant leaks (defined as >1 lb/year).
  • Electrical systems not meeting current local safety codes.
  • Ductwork with leakage greater than 40%.
  • Systems with unauthorized or "jury-rigged" modifications.

2. Safety Requirements & Exclusions

Carbon Monoxide & Combustion Safety

Cracked heat exchangers automatically disqualify equipment. Gas leaks must be repaired before coverage begins. The customer is responsible for installing and maintaining CO detectors. ComfortGuard is not liable for any damages related to CO.

3. Coverage Inclusions & Limitations

The monthly membership fee is $55, totaling $660 annually.

Included Basic Parts (per visit maximum $200)

  • Air filters and belts
  • Contactors and capacitors
  • Thermostats and basic controls

Major Component Coverage

  • Compressors: Customer pays 50% of replacement cost.
  • Motors/Blowers: Covered if under 10 years old.

4. Exclusions & Non-Covered Items

Never Covered

Heat exchanger replacement, ductwork replacement, electrical panel work, code compliance upgrades, environmental contamination cleanup, and consequential damages (e.g., water, mold) are not covered. Commercial or rental property systems are excluded.

5. Parts & Labor Warranty

We provide a 90-day warranty on ComfortGuard-supplied parts and a 30-day labor warranty on installation. Major components have specific warranties (e.g., 1-year parts on compressors). The warranty is void if the system is modified by non-network technicians.

8. Membership Cancellation & Termination

Customers may cancel with 30-day written notice. No refunds are provided for partial months. An equipment assessment fee of $150 may be charged if canceling within the first 60 days of membership. ComfortGuard reserves the right to cancel memberships for reasons including unsafe equipment conditions or non-payment.

ComfortGuard Contractor Network Agreement

Effective Date: August 27, 2025 | Version: 1.0

1. Agreement Overview

Relationship Type

Contractor operates as an independent business, not an employee of ComfortGuard. Participation in the network is voluntary. This is a month-to-month agreement with a 30-day termination notice by either party.

2. Payment Terms & Compensation

Payment Structure

ComfortGuard pays the Contractor standard market rates per service call, with premium rates for emergencies. Payments are made Net 15 days from the submission of a complete invoice.

Invoicing Requirements

Invoices must be submitted within 48 hours of service completion and include the work order number, member information, services performed, parts used, and photo documentation.

3. Service Standards & Performance Requirements

Response Time Standards

A 4-hour maximum response time for emergencies and same or next-day service for priority calls is required. All work must meet local codes and manufacturer specifications.

Customer Service Requirements

A professional appearance, respectful communication, and a clean work area are required at all times.

4. Contractor Requirements & Qualifications

Licensing & Certification

Contractors must maintain a valid state HVAC license, EPA Universal Certification, and carry a minimum of $1M in general liability insurance.

Background & Safety

All technicians must pass a background check and comply with our drug testing program.

5. Termination Conditions

ComfortGuard may terminate this agreement immediately for safety violations, customer complaints, or lapse in insurance coverage. The Contractor may terminate with a 30-day notice.

6. Dispute Resolution & Legal Terms

Disputes will be handled through good faith negotiation, followed by mediation. This agreement is governed by the laws of the state of Michigan.
